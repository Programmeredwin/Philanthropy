Kiambu Integrated Rescue Disabled Organisation prides itself on the provision of basic and finacial needs to the disabled community.

Many lives have been changed for the better after we took initiative to help them

Welcome to Kiambu Integrated Rescue Disabled Organisation, where we believe in empowering individuals with disabilities to lead fulfilling lives. Our organization is dedicated to making a positive impact on the lives of disabled people through various initiatives.



Our Mission

Our mission is to provide support and opportunities for disabled individuals to thrive in their communities. We believe that every person, regardless of their abilities, deserves access to education, employment, and recreational activities.



How We Help

One of the ways we support disabled individuals is by selling soccer balls. Not only does this provide an avenue for recreational activity and social interaction, but it also generates funds that enable us to finance programs and services for the disabled community.

Proceeds from the sale of soccer balls are used to provide financial assistance to disabled individuals for medical treatments, assistive devices, education scholarships, and vocational training. We strive to remove barriers and create opportunities for disabled people to live their lives to the fullest.



Join Us in Making a Difference

We invite you to join us in our mission of empowerment and inclusion. Whether it's purchasing soccer balls, making a donation, or volunteering your time and skills, every contribution helps us make a positive impact in the lives of disabled individuals.
